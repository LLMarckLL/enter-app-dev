# My Favorite Movie: Interstellar

**Genre:** Science Fiction, Drama  
*Directed by:* Christopher Nolan  
*Release Year:* 2014

## Why It’s a Favorite
- Mind-blowing concepts of time and space
- Emotional story with a powerful father-daughter bond
- Breathtaking visuals and soundtrack by Hans Zimmer 🎶

> “Love is the one thing we're capable of perceiving that transcends dimensions of time and space.” – Dr. Brand
